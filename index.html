A love story

Our Story

How We Met

A Wedding, of All Places

Wahito and Obiero met at a wedding — talk of irony. Wahito was trying to teach Obie some dance moves, which he seemed utterly unable to grasp, so he decided to simply laugh his way through. That coincidental encounter led to a whole load of messages — starting from "What is your favourite colour?" all the way to "When did you get born again?"

Growing Together

Deeper Than Expected

The longer the conversations went on, the deeper the connection grew. They discovered they shared not only values but the belief that newborns are not nearly as beautiful as people make them out to be. Some things, it turns out, are a package deal.

The Friendship

Joy, Laughter & a Little Pain

The friendship grew, and eventually it led them to decide that perhaps it would be best if they lived life together, trusting God for the foreseeable future as they intertwined their lives. The journey has been filled with joy, laughter, and some pain — though that last part is, of course, entirely Obie's fault.

Today & Always

For His Glory

All in all, a striving to reflect God's glory in everything they do. So with much joy, they thank God for where He is leading, and pray for more grace as they take this next step — together, before Him, and before the people they love.
